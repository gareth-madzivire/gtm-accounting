Who I Am

Built on integrity, driven by purpose

I am an independent accountant and financial advisor based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, with deep roots across the NGO, property, and manufacturing sectors. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous financial compliance and practical business transformation.

Guided by faith and a commitment to building something that outlasts a single engagement, I approach every client relationship as a long-term partnership. My goal is not just to keep the books — it is to build the financial architecture that allows African businesses to scale with confidence.

Alongside practice, I am building Decima Technologies: a fintech venture developing tax compliance and financial management infrastructure purpose-built for Zimbabwean SMEs.

Compliance-First Philosophy
Every engagement is built on audit-defensible work — ZIMRA-ready, NSSA-compliant, NEC-aligned, and structured to survive scrutiny.
Cross-Sector Depth
From pharmaceutical startups to construction contractors and creative agencies — I bring sector-appropriate financial intelligence to each client.
Technology-Driven Practice
